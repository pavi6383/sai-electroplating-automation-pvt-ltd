Advanced Automation Programming for Electroplating Plants

At Sri Sai Electroplating Automation Pvt Ltd., we specialize in delivering advanced automation programming services tailored specifically for electroplating plants. Our expertise in PLC (Programmable Logic Controller), SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition), and HMI (Human-Machine Interface) programming allows us to create customized automation solutions that enhance operational efficiency, ensure high-quality output, and minimize manual intervention.


Seamless Integration with Existing Systems

We offer seamless integration of automation systems with your existing setups. Whether you are operating a manual or semi-automatic plant, our team can upgrade your systems to achieve full automation. Our process involves

Current System Analysis

Detailed evaluation of your existing processes to identify potential improvements.

Custom Automation Design

Developing a comprehensive automation strategy tailored to your production goals.

Key Benefits Of Automation

Data-Driven Insights

SCADA systems enable real-time monitoring and data logging, facilitating informed decision-making based on comprehensive data.

Improved Safety

Automation reduces the need for human interaction with hazardous materials, enhancing workplace safety.


Quality Control

Automation reduces human error, providing precise control over plating parameters such as temperature, voltage, and chemical concentrations.

Enhanced Efficiency

Automated systems operate continuously with minimal downtime, boosting production rates and ensuring consistent output.

Detailed PLC Programming

Our PLC programming services provide reliable and efficient control for your electroplating processes. We develop customized PLC programs to meet your specific needs, including

Process Control

Ensuring uniform coating and high-quality finishes through precise control of plating parameters.

Sequence Control

Automating process steps to improve operational efficiency and reduce cycle times.

Alarm Management

Real-time monitoring and alerts for deviations from set parameters, ensuring prompt corrective actions

Automated Electroplating Recipe Management

To ensures a precise, consistent plating process. It starts with cleaning the surface, followed by pre-treatment for better adhesion. The metal is then plated onto the substrate using controlled current. After plating, the substrate is rinsed and dried. Finally, automated inspections check for quality. Each step is managed for consistent, high-quality results.

Comprehensive SCADA Solutions

Our SCADA solutions offer extensive supervisory control and data acquisition capabilities, allowing you to monitor and control your electroplating operations from a centralized interface. Key features include

Real-time Monitoring

Continuous tracking of process variables such as temperature, voltage, and chemical concentrations.

Historical Data Logging

Storing and retrieving process data for analysis and reporting.

Remote Access

Monitoring and controlling operations remotely for flexibility and convenience.

Custom Reporting

Generating detailed reports for performance analysis, regulatory compliance, and continuous improvement initiatives.

Intuitive HMI Development

We design user-friendly HMIs that facilitate seamless interaction between operators and automated systems. Our HMI solutions include

Graphical Interfaces

Visual representation of process parameters and statuses for easy monitoring.

Interactive Controls

Touchscreen interfaces for intuitive operation and control of automated systems.

Alarm and Notification Systems

Real-time alerts for process deviations, ensuring prompt responses and corrective actions.

Customization

Tailored interfaces to meet the specific needs of your electroplating processes.
